Procedures

1 : Complete the Admission form

2 : You will get a Email from BUBST Authority and they will tell the date of Viva.

3 : After giving the Viva BUBST Authority will give you a Email. if you passed the Viva then you have to give an Written Exam. if you did not pass the Viva then then you will not be able to give the Written Exam

4 : There will be Units :
A Unit = Science
B Unit = Humanities
C Unit = Commerce.

5 : After giving the Written Exam BUBST Authority will send a Email to you that did you Pass the Written Exam or not. If you Passed the Retain Exam you will get a chance to study in BUBST. But if you dont Pass you will not get a chance to study in BUBST.
