About Us:

Bangladesh University of Business And Science and Technology - BUBST was founded in 1993. with over 12000 students. It is a Public University. The Founder and Chairman is Dr. Md Ariful Islam. The VC of this University is Dr. Mohammed Yunus and Chancellor is Sam Cook. and the Treasurer is Dr. Abdul Goni. the university runs Undergraduate, Graduate, M.phil and PHD Programs. It is one of the best universities in Bangladesh.
